<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Ethereal Frost Minimalism**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Serenity & Silence**: The scene should evoke the quiet of a snow-muffled city.
  2.  **Soft Focus**: Use depth of field and fog to create a dreamlike atmosphere.
  3.  **Cold Warmth**: Predominantly cool tones (blues, whites) with tiny, warm highlights (windows).
  
  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Palette**: Icy Blues (#E0F7FA, #B2EBF2), Deep Slate (#263238), Pure White (#FFFFFF).
  -   **Intent**: To convey the crisp, freezing air of NYC in winter while maintaining a sense of magical calm.
  
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Immersive Full-Screen**: The 3D canvas takes up 100% of the viewport.
  -   **Floating Glass UI**: UI elements (header, text) float above the scene with heavy blur (backdrop-filter) and thin white borders, mimicking ice.
  
  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Procedural Skyline**: Abstracted, towering monoliths for buildings, fading into the snowy fog.
  -   **Particle Snow**: varying sizes of snow particles, some close and out of focus (bokeh).
  -   **Frosted Glass Overlay**: UI containers look like frosted glass.
  
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Gentle Drift**: The camera slowly pans automatically; user interaction is subtle (parallax on mouse move), not aggressive spinning.
  
  **Animation**:
  -   **Slow Motion Snow**: Snow falls slowly, drifting with wind noise.
  -   **Fade-ins**: Elements emerge softly from the fog.
  
  **Typography System**:
  -   **Headings**: *Playfair Display* (Serif) - Elegant, editorial, reminiscent of high-end NYC magazines.
  -   **Body**: *Lato* or *Inter* (Sans-serif) - Clean, legible, modern.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Cyber-Winter Noir**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Contrast & Glow**: Dark buildings against bright snow and neon lights.
  2.  **Geometric Precision**: Sharp edges, low-poly aesthetic.
  3.  **Urban Energy**: The city feels alive even in the snow.
  
  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Palette**: Midnight Blue (#0A192F), Neon Cyan (#64FFDA), Stark White (#F8F9FA).
  -   **Intent**: A more modern, tech-forward interpretation of the city.
  
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Asymmetric Overlay**: Text and UI elements are aligned to a grid but placed asymmetrically to frame the 3D content.
  
  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Glowing Windows**: Buildings have emissive materials for windows.
  -   **Wireframe Accents**: Subtle wireframe overlays on some geometry.
  -   **Digital Snow**: Snowflakes are square pixels or data points.
  
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Active Exploration**: Full OrbitControls allowing the user to fly around the park.
  
  **Animation**:
  -   **Glitch Effects**: Subtle glitches on UI entrance.
  -   **Linear Motion**: Snow falls in straight, fast lines.
  
  **Typography System**:
  -   **Headings**: *Space Mono* or *Roboto Mono* - Technical, precise.
  -   **Body**: *Roboto* - Standard, readable.
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: **Painterly Impressionism**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Brushstroke Textures**: Textures that look hand-painted.
  2.  **Warm Nostalgia**: A slightly sepia or rose-tinted filter over the winter scene.
  3.  **Organic Shapes**: Trees and snow banks are rounded, soft, and natural.
  
  **Color Philosophy**:
  -   **Palette**: Pastel Pink (#FCE4EC), Soft Blue-Grey (#CFD8DC), Cream (#FFF9C4).
  -   **Intent**: To capture the romanticized feeling of a "White Christmas" in New York.
  
  **Layout Paradigm**:
  -   **Frame-within-Frame**: The 3D scene might have a vignette or artistic border.
  
  **Signature Elements**:
  -   **Watercolor Sky**: The skybox is a watercolor gradient.
  -   **Paper-cutout Trees**: Trees might be 2D billboards that always face the camera (billboarding) with painted textures.
  
  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  -   **Storybook**: Scrolling moves the camera along a fixed path, like turning pages.
  
  **Animation**:
  -   **Fluttering**: Snowflakes flutter like petals.
  
  **Typography System**:
  -   **Headings**: *Lora* - Calligraphic roots, poetic.
  -   **Body**: *Merriweather* - Highly readable serif.
</idea>
